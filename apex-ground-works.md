# Apex Ground Works — Company Overview

## Company name

**Apex Ground Works**

---

## What the company is

Apex Ground Works is a professional groundwork and small civil works company focused on **clean, precise, small-to-medium scale exterior projects**.

The company is built around the idea of doing smaller groundwork jobs properly — with attention to detail, clear communication, and a professional finish.

This is **not** a large heavy earthmoving company and **not** a giant infrastructure contractor.

Apex Ground Works is meant to serve clients who need:

- reliable site work
- residential groundwork
- light commercial civil connections
- neat excavation and drainage work
- practical exterior solutions
- retaining walls and landscape-related groundwork

The company should be presented as:

- capable
- trustworthy
- detail-oriented
- practical
- professional
- easy to work with

---

## Main focus of the business

Apex Ground Works focuses on **small, precise, high-quality groundwork services** rather than large-scale excavation or mass earthmoving.

The business is best described as a company for:

- homeowners
- builders
- contractors
- small developers
- property owners
- commercial clients with smaller civil service needs

The work is often custom and site-specific.

## Web design system (Smart Quote baseline)

The **Smart Quote** page (`quote.html`) and shared stylesheet (`css/styles.css`) define the live digital system. All site pages should match that language:

- **Backgrounds:** warm off-whites — `#f7f4ee`, `#f8f5ee`, `#fcfbf8`, `#faf8f2`
- **Text:** `#2d2d2d` / `#1f1f1f` primary; secondary `#5d584f` / `#6d665a`; labels `#7c766a`
- **Accent:** green-teal `#2f6f6a` (primary buttons, focus, highlights) — not a cold SaaS blue
- **Borders:** soft warm lines — `#e5dfd2`, `#ddd4c6`, `#d9d2c5`
- **Shape:** large cards about **26px** radius; controls **14px**; **pill** buttons
- **Tone:** calm typography (DM Sans), generous spacing, soft shadows, minimal noise

The landing page, gallery, contact, and any new forms should reuse these tokens so the experience reads as one product.

## Notes for web and design work

1. This is **not** a massive earthmoving brand.
2. This is **not** a rough, dirty, overly macho excavation company.
3. This is a **clean, modern, trustworthy groundwork business**.
4. Real project visuals should do a lot of the selling.
5. Copy should stay short and practical.
6. The site should feel polished, local, and easy to trust.
7. On the **website**, follow the **Web design system** above (warm neutrals + `#2f6f6a` accent). Print and yard signage can stay legible and high-contrast as needed.
8. Logo and branding should be simple and professional.
